Bhojpur Temple stands so wide,
Carved with care, a source of pride.
Its silent stones, its open dome,
Make every visitor feel at home.

The Upper Lake, with waters clear,
Reflects the skies we hold so dear.
It flows with tales from days gone by,
Beneath the sun and evening sky.

In Bhimbetka, the walls still speak,
Of hunters brave and artists meek.
Their painted stories, old yet bright,
Still glow with life in morning light.
